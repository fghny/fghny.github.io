Flushing Gospel Hall

Christians gathered together unto the name of the Lord Jesus Christ at Flushing, New York

Welcome

We are an assembly of Christians living in the borough of Queens in New York City and the surrounding communities, who “gather together in the name of the Lord Jesus Christ” and claim the Lord’s promise:

"For where two or three are gathered together in my name, there am I in the midst of them"

Matthew 18:20


Please explore our site and come visit us. For more information see what we believe or learn more about our meetings.
